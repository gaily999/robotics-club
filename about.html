About Us

We are a vibrant community of robotics enthusiasts and pioneers from Manolo Fortich NHS, driven by a shared passion for innovation and technology.

Who We Are

The Manolo Fortich National High School NanoNova Robotics Club is a student-led club dedicated to fostering creativity, innovation, and technical excellence through technology. We provide a collaborative environment where students can explore programming, robotics, multimedia production, and graphic design while developing practical skills. Our club encourages curiosity, teamwork, and continuous learning by engaging members in hands-on projects, workshops, competitions, and community initiatives. Together, we strive to inspire the next generation of innovators, problem-solvers, and technology leaders.

Core Values

Innovation

We embrace creativity and continuously seek new ideas, technologies, and solutions to solve real-world challenges.

Leadership

We empower members to take initiative, inspire others, and contribute positively to the growth of the club and the community.

Teamwork

We believe that collaboration, respect, and effective communication are essential to achieving shared goals and creating meaningful projects.

Integrity

We uphold honesty, responsibility, and professionalism in everything we do, fostering a culture of trust and accountability.

What We Do

Programming

Members develop websites, applications, and software projects while enhancing their coding, logical thinking, and problem-solving abilities.

Competitions

We participate in robotics and technology-related competitions that challenge our skills, encourage innovation, and promote teamwork.
